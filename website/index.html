LifeReverseEngineering

Deep research pipeline for Learn, Earn, and IDEAS.

Single-Copy · Evidence-Aware · Iterative

LRE converts profile context into practical outputs: reading plans, investment thesis drafts, and research directions, then syncs latest artifacts into dedicated repos.

Open Repository Live Domain

Repo Tracks

Three tracks map outcomes into maintainable sub-repositories.

Learn

Book recommendations, skill paths, and learning notes for long-term growth.

Earn

Investment ideas, risk framing, and thesis updates for decision support.

IDEAS

Research topics, proposal drafts, and follow-up milestones.

Pipeline

Run stages are chained to keep context handoff and output quality stable.

1) Profile Build identity/evidence baseline and confidence mapping.
2) Books Generate books aligned to strengths, weaknesses, and growth goals.
3) Investments Draft opportunities and risk-aware thesis summaries.
4) Ideas Create research and product direction candidates.
